About Us

Excellence, built on faith

Mohammadiya Educational Institute offers a state-of-the-art, three-storey campus where students immerse themselves in rich Islamic teaching alongside a comprehensive modern curriculum. Prayer is observed on time in a calm, purposeful environment, and hands-on computer literacy prepares students for the world beyond the classroom.

Our aim is simple: help every student thrive academically and spiritually, and leave Mohammadiya ready to carry both knowledge and character into their lives.
